  1. Click **Start**
  2. Click **Control Panel**
  3. Click **Network and Internet Connections**
  4. Click **Network Connections**
  5. **Right-click** your Internet **connection** and click **Properties**
  6. Click on **Advanced** tab up the top of the dialog
  7. Click **Settings**
  8. Go to **Exceptions**
