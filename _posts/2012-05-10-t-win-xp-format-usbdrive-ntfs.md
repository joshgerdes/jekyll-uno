Windows XP to format USB Flash drives as NTFS

1. Right click My Computer
  
2. Click Properties
  
3. Click Hardware
  
4. Click Device Manager
  
5. Click Disk Drives and right click your USB device.
  
6. Click Properties
  
7. Click Policies
  
8. Click Optimize for performance.
  
9. Click Apply
  
10. Restart Computer
