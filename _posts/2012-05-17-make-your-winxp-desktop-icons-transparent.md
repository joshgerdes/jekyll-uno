  1. Right Click an empty space on the Desktop
  2. Click Properties
  3. Click the Desktop tab
  4. Click Customize Desktop
  5. Click on Desktop
  6. Uncheck Lock Desktop Items
  7. Click Ok
  8. Click Apply
