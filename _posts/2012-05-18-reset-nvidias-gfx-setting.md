  1. Click **Start**
  2. Click **Programs**/All Programs
  3. Click **NVidia**
  4. Click Nvidia **Control** **Panel**
  5. Select **Manage** **3D** settings
  6. Click the **Global Settings** tab
  7. Click **Restore**
  8. Click Ok
