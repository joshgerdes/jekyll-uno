_Getting a period after each word in Microsoft Office? That is due to formatting marks, being enabled. To disable them follow the instructions below:_

  1. Open **Word**
  2. Click **Tools**
  3. Click **Options**
  4. View and **untick** the **SPACES** box
  5. Click **ok**
