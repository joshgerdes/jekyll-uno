  1. Click **Start**
  2. Click **Control Panel**
  3. Open **Network and Sharing Center**
  4. Click **Manage Wireless Networks** _(to the left)_
  5. Right click and **remove** your wireless **network** from the list
  6. **Attempt** re-**connection**.
