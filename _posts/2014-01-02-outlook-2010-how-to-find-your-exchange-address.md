  1. Open **Outlook** 
  2. Click on **File** 
  3. Click on **Account Settings** 
  4. Click **Account Settings** 
  5. Select the mailbox you would like to retrieve the Exchange address of and select Change
  6. Your Exchange address is titled under the Server field
