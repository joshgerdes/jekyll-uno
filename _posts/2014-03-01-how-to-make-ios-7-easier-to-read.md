  1. Open **Settings** 
  2. Select **General** 
  3. Select **Accessibility
  
** 
  4. Swipe to **Enable Bold Text** 
  5. Select and **swipe** the **Larger**
			  
    **Type** option to adjust text size. 
  6. Select and **swipe** the **Increase**
			  
    **Contrast** option to make the text easier to read.
