---

1. Open **Settings** 
2. Select **General** 
3. Select **Accessibility
4. Swipe to **Enable Bold Text** 
5. Select and **swipe** the **Larger**
6. **Type** option to adjust text size. 
7 . Select and **swipe** the **Increase**
8.  **Contrast** option to make the text easier to read.
