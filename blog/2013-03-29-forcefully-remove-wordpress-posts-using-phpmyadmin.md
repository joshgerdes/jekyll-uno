&nbsp;

  1. Launch PHPMyAdmin _(either directly or through your web hosting control panel &#8211; doesn&#8217;t matter)_.
  2. Go into the wordpress database
  3. Go into your: **Posts** table
  4. Click **Operations**
  5. Click **Empty the table (TRUNCATE)**
  6. Click Ok
